Description

KDA Indoor Tennis is a state-of-the-art sports arena located in Batam Island, Indonesia. It is a popular destination for tennis enthusiasts, both amateurs and professionals. The arena features six indoor courts, making it the largest indoor tennis facility in Batam Island. The courts are made of high-quality material, which provides a comfortable playing experience. One of the fun facts about KDA Indoor Tennis is that it is the only tennis arena in Batam Island that provides tennis training for kids.
